Outdoors

  • Nearby Lakes
    • Explore one of the many lakes in the cities. Many have paths for walking or riding bikes.
  • Municipal Hiker Club
    • Plans 2 hikes of varying lengths scheduled each week
    • This includes weekend outings, vacation trips, and social events
    • $15 for a yearly membership
    • Call (612) 661-4875 for more info
  • North Commons Water Park - $6.00 for people 42 inches and taller, $5.00 for people under 42 inches. *$2.00 off after 6:00 p.m. Mon-Fri only. Outdoor water park with slides for kids and adults.
    1520 Johnson St NE Northeast Minneapolis (612) 370-4937.
  • Richfield Pool - 630 East 66th Street (just east of Portland Ave on 66th St), Richfield, MN 55423, (612) 861-9350
  • Minnesota has thousands of hiking trails, including right within the Twin Cities. Examples include trails in Theodore Wirth Park, Three Rivers Park District, Minnehaha Regional Park, Swede Hollow Park, Fort Snelling Park, and Hyland Lake Park Reserve.
  • Cross-country skiing - there are a lot of groomed trails in the parks system, and you can rent skis, boots, and a ski pass at the Theodore Wirth Park Loppet Foundation.
    1301 Theodore Wirth Pkwy Minneapolis, MN 55422 (612) 604-5330
