Affordable Therapy

  • PsychologyToday
    • PsychologyToday has filters that include insurance, and any specific areas of focus or types of therapy. They also group by zip code, so providers will be in a convenient area.
    • On the back of the Insurance Card, call Member Services. You can ask them to look for therapists in your area that are completely covered by insurance.

Affordable Clothing

  • Hennepin County

    • Harriet's Closet - In Minneapolis, once a month you can call and schedule a time to pick out 3 outfits. Phone Number (612) 825-3333
    • Sabathani's Clothing Closet and Food Distribution Center - Schedule Online, there is an intake process for new people! Website to schedule here.

    Ramsey County

    • Joseph's Coat, St. Paul - Once a month you call and schedule a time to go in and fill up two bags with clothing. NO CHARGE. Phone Number: (651) 291-2472
    • Neighbors Clothes Closet - Very cheap prices. Located at 222 Grand Ave W, South St Paul, MN 55075.
